Analysis

African journalists earn less than their white counterparts

Male journalist are slightly better paid on average than female journalists. Male sub-editors seem to be better paid that female sub-editors but we don't have much data to back this up.

Distribution of jobs across races

If you're white and you earn an above average wage - you tend to think that South African journalism is in crisis. If you're white and you earn less than the average wage - you don't think so. Africans don't have the same distribution - they mostly think that there is a crisis regardless of wage.

Africans are mostly journalists. Whites are mostly editors.

People mostly keep the same job titles - but there is some migration.

If you're in online, you're often in print too. If you're in print, you are sometimes in online.
